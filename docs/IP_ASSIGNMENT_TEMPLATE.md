# IP Assignment Template

> **Important:** This is a practical starter template, not legal advice. Replace the placeholders and have a qualified lawyer review it for your jurisdiction before signing.

## Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (the **Agreement**) is made on **[DATE]** between:

- **Assignor:** **Joshua Owusu** of **[ADDRESS]**
- **Assignee:** **Tish Group** of **[REGISTERED ADDRESS]**

### 1. Background

The Assignor created and developed certain software, source code, designs, documentation, branding materials, product assets, and related intellectual property associated with the product known as **TillFlow** (the **Works**).

The parties intend that all right, title, and interest in the Works shall belong to the Assignee.

### 2. Assignment

The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Assignee, with full title guarantee, all worldwide right, title, and interest in and to the Works, including:

- all copyright and related rights
- all source code and object code
- all software architecture, database structures, and technical documentation
- all product designs, interfaces, graphics, text, and brand assets created by the Assignor for TillFlow
- all updates, enhancements, derivative works, and related materials created before the date of this Agreement
- all rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement relating to the Works

### 3. Further Assurances

The Assignor agrees to sign any further documents and do any reasonable acts needed to confirm, perfect, or record the Assignee's ownership of the Works.

### 4. Moral Rights

To the extent permitted by law, the Assignor waives and agrees not to assert any moral rights or similar rights in the Works against the Assignee or its successors.

### 5. Consideration

The Assignor acknowledges receipt of good and valuable consideration for this assignment, the sufficiency of which is acknowledged by the parties.

> Optional wording if needed: "The consideration for this assignment is [AMOUNT / SHARES / OTHER VALUE]."

### 6. Warranties

The Assignor warrants that:

- the Assignor is the original creator of the Works except for third-party components properly used under valid licenses
- the Assignor has the right to assign the Works
- to the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Works do not infringe the rights of any third party except for disclosed third-party materials
- the Works are free from any undisclosed liens, assignments, or encumbrances

### 7. Third-Party Components

Any third-party software, libraries, frameworks, fonts, or assets used in the Works remain subject to their own license terms and are excluded from this assignment except to the extent the Assignor has rights in any modifications or integrations created by the Assignor.

### 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of **[JURISDICTION]**.

### 9. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the assignment of the Works and supersedes prior discussions or understandings on that subject.
