# Intra-Group IP Use Memo

> **Important:** This is a practical internal starter document, not legal advice. Use it as an internal memo or basis for a more formal intra-group IP license or authorization, and have counsel review it for your jurisdiction and tax/corporate structure.

## Purpose

This memo records the intended internal arrangement under which:

- **Tish Group** owns the intellectual property relating to **TillFlow**; and
- **TillFlow Technologies** is authorized to use, operate, develop, market, support, and commercialize TillFlow as the operating entity within the group.

## Parties

- **IP Owner:** Tish Group
- **Operating Entity:** TillFlow Technologies

## Background

1. The intellectual property associated with TillFlow, including software, source code, product designs, documentation, branding materials, user interface assets, technical documentation, deployment materials, and related works (the **TillFlow IP**) is intended to be owned by **Tish Group**.
2. TillFlow Technologies is the group entity designated to build, operate, distribute, support, market, and commercialize the TillFlow product.
3. The parties want a clear internal record confirming that operational use by TillFlow Technologies does not change legal ownership of the TillFlow IP.

## Ownership Statement

All right, title, and interest in the TillFlow IP is and shall remain vested in **Tish Group**, unless expressly assigned in writing by Tish Group.

TillFlow Technologies acknowledges that its use of the TillFlow IP does not transfer ownership and does not create any ownership interest in the TillFlow IP except as may be expressly agreed in writing.

## Authorized Use by TillFlow Technologies

Tish Group authorizes TillFlow Technologies to use the TillFlow IP for the following internal and commercial purposes:

- develop, maintain, improve, and adapt the TillFlow product;
- deploy and host the TillFlow platform;
- market, promote, demo, and sell TillFlow products and services;
- enter into customer, reseller, support, or implementation arrangements connected to TillFlow;
- create and distribute customer-facing documentation, onboarding material, product copy, and training material;
- use the TillFlow brand, product assets, and related materials in the ordinary course of operating the business;
- appoint employees, contractors, or service providers to work on TillFlow on behalf of TillFlow Technologies, subject to appropriate confidentiality and IP assignment terms.

## Restrictions

TillFlow Technologies may not, except with written approval from Tish Group:

- assign, transfer, sell, or pledge the TillFlow IP outside the group;
- grant exclusive rights in the TillFlow IP to third parties;
- represent to third parties that TillFlow Technologies is the legal owner of the TillFlow IP where that is not the case;
- dispose of core TillFlow IP assets outside ordinary business operations.

## Third-Party Components

Any third-party software, frameworks, fonts, libraries, APIs, and assets used within TillFlow remain subject to their own license terms. This memo applies only to the rights held by the group in the TillFlow IP and does not override third-party license obligations.

## Confidentiality

TillFlow Technologies shall take reasonable steps to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and security of the TillFlow IP and related internal materials, including through appropriate controls for employees, contractors, vendors, repositories, hosting platforms, and backups.

## Revenue and Commercial Operations

Unless otherwise documented in a separate intra-group services, royalty, or licensing arrangement, TillFlow Technologies may conduct customer-facing commercial operations for TillFlow as the operating entity.

If the group later chooses to formalize royalties, service fees, or transfer-pricing arrangements, those matters should be addressed in separate finance and legal documentation.

## Term and Review

This memo remains in effect until replaced, amended, or withdrawn in writing by the group.

The parties may later replace this memo with a more formal:

- intra-group IP license agreement;
- brand license;
- technology services agreement; or
- broader intercompany operating agreement.

## Non-Public Nature

This memo is intended for internal governance and record-keeping. It does not need to be publicly disclosed unless required by law, financing, audit, due diligence, or a specific contractual process.
