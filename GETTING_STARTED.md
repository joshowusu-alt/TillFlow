# TillFlow - Getting Started Guide

Welcome to TillFlow! This guide will help you set up your business and start selling in minutes.

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Access Your Dashboard

1. Open your browser and go to your POS URL
2. Log in with the default owner account:
   - **Email:** `owner@demo.com`
   - **Password:** `Pass1234!`

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** Change this password immediately after first login!

---

### Step 2: Configure Your Business

Go to **Settings** (in the sidebar under Administration):

1. **Business Name** - Enter your supermarket/shop name
2. **Currency** - Select your currency (GHS for Ghana Cedi, NGN for Naira, etc.)
3. **VAT Enabled** - Toggle if you charge VAT/GST
4. Click **Save Changes**

---

### Step 3: Add Your Products

Go to **Products** in the sidebar:

1. Click **Add Product**
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** - Product name (e.g., "Coca-Cola 500ml")
   - **Barcode** - Scan or type the barcode
   - **Selling Price** - The price customers pay
   - **Cost Price** - Your purchase price (for profit tracking)
3. Click **Save**

üí° **Tip:** Add your top 20 products first, then add more as you go!

---

### Step 4: Create Staff Accounts

Go to **Users** in the sidebar (Owner only):

1. Click **Add User**
2. Add your cashiers with the **CASHIER** role
3. Add managers with the **MANAGER** role

| Role | Can Do |
|------|--------|
| **Cashier** | POS, view products |
| **Manager** | + Sales, inventory, reports |
| **Owner** | + Settings, users, backups |

---

### Step 5: Start Selling!

Go to **POS** in the sidebar:

1. **Scan or search** for products
2. **Adjust quantities** as needed
3. Click **Checkout**
4. Select **payment method** (Cash, Card, Mobile Money)
5. **Print receipt** or email to customer

---

## Advanced Setup

Products will show low stock warnings when inventory drops below threshold.

Go to **Settings ‚Üí Data Backup** to:
- Export all your data (recommended weekly)
- Import data when migrating
